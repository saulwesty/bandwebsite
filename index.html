THE 49ERS
About

Who we are

The 49ers are a popular South London covers band, specialising in ska, punk and new wave classics.

What we play

We bring for your noisy entertainment the music of our long lost youth, spanning the late 70s all the way up to the early 1980s. At a 49ers gig, you can look forward to classics from Blondie, the Specials, Elvis Costello, Madness and many more.
